Private Tours in Paris

Paris, the City of Light, offers an unparalleled tapestry of art, history, and culture. From the iron lattice of the Eiffel Tower to the masterpieces of the Louvre, every corner tells a story waiting to be discovered. At MyPrivateTours.com, our expert Parisian guides unlock the city beyond the tourist trail — from hidden passages in Le Marais to the best croissant in Saint-Germain-des-Pres. Our private tours mean no waiting in long queues, no rushing through galleries, and no sharing your experience with strangers. Whether you're standing beneath the stained glass of Sainte-Chapelle or cruising the Seine at sunset, every moment is crafted around you. We offer flexible scheduling, free cancellation up to 48 hours before your tour, and instant booking confirmation. With skip-the-line access to Paris's most popular attractions and local knowledge that only comes from years of guiding, MyPrivateTours.com is your gateway to an authentic, unforgettable Paris experience.

Frequently Asked Questions About Paris Tours

How do I book a private tour in Paris?

Simply choose your preferred tour, select your date and group size, and book instantly through our secure platform. You'll receive a confirmation email with all details, including your guide's name and meeting point.

What is your cancellation policy for Paris tours?

All our Paris tours offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before the scheduled start time. For last-minute changes, contact our support team and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Are entrance tickets included in the tour price?

Yes, all entrance fees and skip-the-line tickets are included in the price for tours that visit ticketed attractions such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre, and Versailles.

What languages do your Paris guides speak?

Our Paris guides are fluent in English, and many also speak French, Spanish, German, and Italian. Please note your language preference when booking.

Can I customise the itinerary?

Absolutely. One of the main benefits of a private tour is flexibility. Let your guide know your interests and they'll tailor the experience to your group.
