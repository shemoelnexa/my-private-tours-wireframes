Tour Overview

Experience the Eiffel Tower like a true insider with our premium private tour. Your expert Parisian guide will lead you past the queues with priority skip-the-line access, sharing fascinating stories about Gustave Eiffel's engineering masterpiece as you ascend to the summit. Enjoy breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views of Paris from 276 metres above the city, visit the recreated office of Gustave Eiffel, and learn about the tower's remarkable history from controversial eyesore to beloved global icon.

What's Included

  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Skip-the-line priority access
  • Eiffel Tower entrance ticket (2nd floor + summit)
  • Small group (max 8 people)
  • Panoramic city orientation from the summit
  • Historical commentary and insider stories

What's Not Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off
  • Food and beverages
  • Gratuities (optional)

Highlights

  • Available morning, afternoon, and sunset time slots

Itinerary

1

Meeting Point — Trocadero Gardens

Meet your guide at the Trocadero esplanade for the best opening view of the Eiffel Tower. Your guide will give a brief history of the tower's construction and the 1889 World's Fair.

15 minutes
2

Skip-the-Line Entry & Second Floor

Bypass the public queues and enter through the priority access gate. Ascend to the second floor where your guide will point out Paris's major landmarks — Notre-Dame, Sacre-Coeur, Arc de Triomphe, and the Seine.

45 minutes
3

Summit Access

Take the elevator to the summit at 276 metres. Visit Gustave Eiffel's private office, see the champagne bar, and enjoy unobstructed 360-degree views across the entire Paris region.

45 minutes
4

Descent & Final Stories

Descend at your own pace. Your guide will share final stories about the tower's role in radio transmission, its near-demolition, and the nightly light show. Tour ends at ground level.

30 minutes

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Eiffel Tower entrance ticket included?

Yes, your tour includes skip-the-line entrance tickets to the second floor and summit of the Eiffel Tower.

What language does the guide speak?

All our Eiffel Tower tour guides are fluent in English. Guides speaking French, Spanish, and German are available upon request.

Can I customise the itinerary?

Yes. While the core route covers the main highlights, your guide can adjust the pace and focus based on your interests.

What happens in bad weather?

The Eiffel Tower operates in most weather conditions. In the rare event of severe weather closure, we will reschedule or provide a full refund.
