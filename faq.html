Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and enjoying your private tour

Booking

  • Choose your tour, select a date and group size, and book instantly through our secure platform. You'll receive an email confirmation with your guide's details and meeting point within minutes.

  • We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely.

  • Yes. All bookings receive instant confirmation. You'll get an email with your booking reference, guide name, and meeting point details.

  • Subject to availability, we accept bookings up to 24 hours in advance. For peak season, we recommend booking at least 1 week ahead.

Cancellation & Refunds

  • Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your tour start time. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable.

  • Log in to your booking confirmation email and click 'Manage Booking', or contact our support team via WhatsApp or email.

  • Most tours operate rain or shine. For severe weather that affects safety, we'll reschedule at no extra charge or provide a full refund.

Guides & Experience

  • All guides are fluent in English. Many also speak Spanish, French, German, Italian, or Portuguese. Note your preference when booking.

  • Yes, 100%. You will never be grouped with strangers. It's just you, your party, and your dedicated guide.

  • Absolutely. Private tours are flexible by nature. Discuss your interests with your guide, and they'll tailor the experience to your group.

What's Included

  • For tours visiting ticketed attractions (e.g. Eiffel Tower, Colosseum, Vatican), entrance tickets and skip-the-line access are included in the tour price.

  • Walking tours do not include transport. For tours that include transport (e.g. Versailles day trip), this is clearly stated on the tour page.

  • Food tours include tastings as described. Other tours do not include meals, but your guide can recommend excellent local restaurants.
