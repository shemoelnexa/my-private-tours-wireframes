About Eiffel Tower Tours

The Eiffel Tower stands as Paris's most recognised landmark and the world's most-visited paid monument. Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, Gustave Eiffel's iron masterpiece rises 330 metres above the Champ de Mars, offering sweeping panoramic views of the entire city.

A private Eiffel Tower tour transforms a standard visit into something extraordinary. Skip the notoriously long queues — often exceeding two hours in peak season — and gain priority access with your expert guide. Learn the fascinating stories behind its construction, the controversies it sparked, and the engineering innovations that keep it standing.

Our guides share insider knowledge about the best viewing angles, the hidden apartment at the summit, and the sparkling light show that illuminates Paris every evening. Whether you choose a morning tour for the clearest views or a sunset tour for golden-hour photography, your private Eiffel Tower experience will be one of the highlights of your Paris visit.
