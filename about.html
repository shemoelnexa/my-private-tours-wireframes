About MyPrivateTours

Premium private tours crafted by experts who love what they do

Our Story

MyPrivateTours was founded with a simple belief: the best way to experience a city is through the eyes of someone who truly knows it. What began as a single guide sharing their passion for Paris has grown into a network of expert local guides across eight of the world's most captivating cities.

We don't do mass-market. Every tour is private — just you, your group, and a guide who lives and breathes their city. From skip-the-line access at the Eiffel Tower to hidden gems in Venice's back canals, we curate experiences that go far beyond what a guidebook can offer.

Our guides aren't just knowledgeable — they're storytellers, historians, and food lovers who turn a city visit into a lifelong memory.
